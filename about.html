On our platform, you can donate to a nonprofit with confidence. Our robust due diligence process covers legal, compliance, financial and impact checks for each donation made to any non-profit, including in-person visits to beneficiaries and actual feedback reports. We do all this so that our donors can trust that their giving is indeed doing what it's supposed to do - change lives of children and give them a chance to grow up in a family.

We believe in collaboration as we know this work is bigger than any one organization. We are part of a global network of nonprofit organizations leading the worldwide movement.
